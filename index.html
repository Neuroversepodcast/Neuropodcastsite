About the Podcast

The Neuroverse is a casual, honest podcast centered around autistic experiences — the good, the awkward, and the hilarious. We invite guests from all walks of life to talk about identity, daily life, special interests, and whatever else the conversation accidentally turns into. No serious vibes, just relatable content.
